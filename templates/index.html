    📝 Quick Tips

    • To lock a Break/Lunch period, write BREAK in the corresponding column across all classes.
    • To assign multiple faculty to a lab, separate their names with commas (e.g., Smith, Jones).
    • You may leave the "Class" column blank in subsequent rows; the system will auto-fill it downwards.
